FAQ

Honest answers.

Will you ever charge commission on my bookings?

Never. Not on new clients, returning clients, or any booking at all. You pay one flat monthly fee and keep 100% of every booking. This is our core promise and it will never change.

How does the SMS reminder limit work exactly?

Your plan includes SMS reminders for up to 300 bookings per month (that's 600 SMS — 2 per booking). This covers the vast majority of businesses. If you exceed 300 bookings in a month, we charge $0.05 per SMS above that threshold. For a 1,000-booking month, that's about $35 extra. We notify you before any overage is charged. Email reminders are always unlimited and free.

Does the Arabic really work properly?

Yes — built natively, not translated. The full dashboard, your customer booking page, and all reminder messages work in Arabic with proper right-to-left layout and Gulf Arabic tone. Tested with business owners across UAE, KSA, and Kuwait.

Do I need any tech skills to set this up?

No. If you can fill in a form, you can run Zenda. Add your services, set your hours, share your link. The whole setup takes 20 minutes and there is nothing that requires a developer or an agency.

Can I use Zenda for multiple locations or staff?

Yes. Starter covers 2 staff. Growth covers 10. Pro covers unlimited staff and multiple locations. You can switch plans at any time with no penalty and no lock-in.

What if I want to cancel?

Cancel any time from your dashboard in one click — no forms, no phone calls, no questions. You keep access until the end of your billing period. If the service does not work as described in your first 30 days, we will refund you in full.

Is Zenda GCC VAT compliant?

Yes. Set your VAT registration number and Zenda handles VAT display on invoices and booking confirmations correctly for UAE, Saudi Arabia, and other GCC countries with a 15% or 5% VAT rate.

What is coming in future phases?

Phase 2 (Q3 2026) adds WhatsApp campaigns, loyalty programmes, automated review collection, and Instagram booking buttons. Phase 3 adds full POS, inventory, and multi-location management. Your current plan price is locked in — no surprise upgrades ever.

SMS reminders are included in your plan for up to 300 bookings per month (600 SMS total — 2 reminders per booking). This covers the vast majority of businesses. Example: A business with 1,000 bookings in a month uses 2,000 SMS. The first 600 (300 bookings × 2) are included. The remaining 1,400 SMS are charged at $0.05 each = $70 overage. Total for that month: your plan fee + $70. We will always notify you in advance.
